Who we are

Born from lived experience. Built for lasting change.

Creating Executive Opportunities Foundation was founded by a survivor who understands firsthand how adverse childhood experiences — ACEs — shape a life. That experience became a mission: to make sure no child, family, or aspiring leader has to navigate trauma without support, knowledge, or community.

"My background in psychology, my faith, and my own survival gave me a lens that credentials alone cannot teach. We don't just build programs — we build people."
— Founder, Creating Executive Opportunities Foundation

We work at the intersection of trauma-informed care, community resilience, and leadership development — equipping churches, nonprofits, and individuals with the tools to create real, measurable change.

Our pillars

Educate Training churches, nonprofits, and HR teams in trauma-informed practices and ACE prevention frameworks.
Empower Giving aspiring leaders — especially women and survivors — the tools, coaching, and community to lead well.
Stabilize Supporting single mothers on their journey toward financial and emotional stability — a program currently in development and coming soon.
Invest in children Annual community drives and youth programs ensure every child has what they need to learn and grow.

What we do

Programs built for real people.

Each program addresses a specific gap — and together they form a complete ecosystem of support from childhood through leadership.

Trauma & Faith

Trauma-Informed Church Program Builder

We partner with churches and faith-based organizations to help them build internal programs that identify, respond to, and prevent adverse childhood experiences — grounded in both clinical best practice and Scripture.

Single Moms · Coming Soon

Get Well Community

A dedicated support community for single mothers seeking financial, emotional, and personal stability. We are building this program with intention — designed to meet women where they are and walk with them toward where they deserve to be. Stay connected for launch updates.

Leadership

Creating Executive Opportunities

Coaching, mentorship, and community for aspiring leaders who have been overlooked, under-resourced, or underestimated. We help you build the career you were created for — from the inside out.

Mental Help Podcast

Real conversations about healing, faith, and leading well.

Hosted by the founder of Creating Executive Opportunities Foundation — combining a master's in psychology, lived experience as an ACEs survivor, and a deep faith to explore mental health without the stigma. Episodes launching soon.
